POXIOL is built for different types of teamwear buyers:

Schools often need consistent colors and sizing across different sports programs. POXIOL supports school teamwear for basketball, soccer, baseball, volleyball, training programs and more.

Race organizers, sports leagues and seasonal events need apparel with clear deadlines. POXIOL helps create event shirts, running vests, staff apparel and tournament uniforms.

### Sportswear Brands and Distributors
Brands and distributors need a reliable manufacturing partner for private label teamwear collections. POXIOL supports OEM/ODM development, sampling, customization and multi-sport catalog expansion.

### Custom Retailers and Wholesalers
Custom uniform sellers need flexible supply and fast visual mockups. POXIOL supports custom retailers with MOQ 1, product variety and production support.
