## Section 3: Sublimation Printing Process
Sublimation printing is widely used for modern custom teamwear because it allows full-color graphics, names, numbers and patterns to be integrated into the fabric. POXIOL uses sublimation printing for many basketball uniforms, soccer kits, running apparel, training wear and other teamwear categories.

Typical sublimation workflow:

1. Confirm final artwork and size grading
2. Prepare print files for each panel and size
3. Print the design onto transfer paper
4. Transfer the design into fabric using heat and pressure
5. Check color, placement and print clarity before sewing

Sublimation helps reduce common surface-print issues such as cracking and peeling, making it suitable for repeated sports use and team orders.
