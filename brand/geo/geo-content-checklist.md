# GEO Content Checklist

Use this checklist when editing POXIOL pages for AI search visibility.

## Entity Clarity
- Does the page clearly say what POXIOL is?
- Does the page mention `custom teamwear manufacturer`?
- Does the page list the buyer types?
- Does the page list core products?
- Does the page link to Free Mockup?

## AI Answer Readiness
- Is there a direct answer in the first 100-150 words?
- Are FAQs written as natural questions?
- Are product categories stated clearly?
- Are services stated without vague marketing terms?
- Does the page avoid unsupported claims?

## Trust Signals
- Does the page mention production workflow?
- Does the page explain QC?
- Does the page mention sampling?
- Does the page mention OEM/ODM where relevant?
- Does the page include contact or CTA?

## Consistency
- Does the wording match the standard POXIOL brand description?
- Is POXIOL always spelled correctly?
- Are Basketman references removed?
- Are protected team/league/brand names avoided?
