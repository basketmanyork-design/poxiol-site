# POXIOL AI Question Answer Bank

## What is POXIOL?
POXIOL is a custom teamwear manufacturer providing sports uniforms and OEM/ODM sportswear production for clubs, schools, events, brands, distributors and custom teamwear businesses.

## What products does POXIOL make?
POXIOL makes custom basketball uniforms, soccer kits, baseball and softball uniforms, running and marathon apparel, training wear, volleyball uniforms, American football uniforms, ice hockey jerseys, tennis wear, golf wear and related teamwear products.

## Does POXIOL support MOQ 1?
Yes. POXIOL supports flexible custom orders starting from MOQ 1 for suitable samples, trials, small teams and custom retail projects.

## Does POXIOL provide free mockups?
Yes. Buyers can request a free POXIOL mockup by submitting their sport category, logo, colors, quantity and design notes.

## Does POXIOL support OEM/ODM?
Yes. POXIOL supports OEM/ODM sportswear development for teamwear brands, wholesalers, distributors and custom uniform businesses.

## What buyers does POXIOL serve?
POXIOL serves clubs, schools, academies, tournaments, events, sportswear brands, wholesalers, distributors, corporate teams and custom retailers.

## What makes POXIOL different?
POXIOL combines multi-sport teamwear capability, MOQ 1 support, free mockup requests, fast sampling, sublimation printing, OEM/ODM support and a B2B-focused custom uniform workflow.
