## Who We Are
POXIOL is a custom teamwear manufacturer focused on helping global buyers create professional sports uniforms for teams, clubs, schools, tournaments, events, distributors and sportswear brands.

We provide multi-sport custom apparel solutions across basketball, soccer, baseball, running, training wear, volleyball, American football, ice hockey, tennis and golf. From a single sample request to a full OEM/ODM teamwear collection, POXIOL is built to support both small custom projects and scalable teamwear programs.

Our work is not limited to printing logos on blank apparel. We support the full custom teamwear process, including design direction, visual mockup, fabric selection, sample development, sublimation printing, name and number customization, production, quality checking and delivery support.

## What Makes POXIOL Different

### Free Mockup Support
POXIOL helps buyers start faster by offering a free mockup request process. Buyers can submit a sport category, logo, colors, quantity and design notes, and the POXIOL team will review the project details and help prepare the next step.

### MOQ 1 Flexible Orders
Not every buyer starts with a large order. POXIOL supports flexible custom orders from MOQ 1, making it easier to test a sample, create a proof of concept, serve a small team or validate a custom retail product before scaling.

### Fast Sampling
For many custom teamwear projects, sample production can be arranged quickly after design confirmation. This helps teams and brands review fabric, fit, print effect and construction before bulk production.

### Multi-Sport Capability
POXIOL is not limited to one sport. We support a wide range of teamwear categories, which makes it easier for buyers to manage multiple sports through one supplier.

### OEM/ODM Ready
For brands, distributors and custom teamwear businesses, POXIOL supports OEM/ODM development including original design, private label planning, sampling, production and category expansion.

### Performance Fabric and Sublimation Printing
POXIOL focuses on performance-driven teamwear. We support sublimation printing, breathable fabrics, quick-dry options, stretch and mobility considerations, and QC inspection before shipment.
