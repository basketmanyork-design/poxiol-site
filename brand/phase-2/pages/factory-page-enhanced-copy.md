## Production Workflow

### 1. Project Review
Every custom teamwear project starts with understanding the buyer's sport category, product type, quantity, color requirements, logo placement, player names, numbers and target delivery timeline.

For OEM/ODM projects, we also review brand positioning, product range, fabric preferences, packaging needs and repeat-order plans.

### 2. Design and Mockup Confirmation
Before sampling or production, POXIOL helps buyers confirm the visual direction of the custom uniform. This may include team color direction, logo placement, name and number layout, pattern style, product type selection and front/back visual mockup.

### 3. Fabric and Material Preparation
Fabric selection depends on sport category, buyer preference and use case. Basketball and running apparel may require lightweight breathable fabric, while training wear may require more structured materials for warm-up and travel use.

### 4. Sublimation Printing
Sublimation printing is commonly used for custom teamwear because it allows full-color graphics to be integrated into the fabric. It is suitable for basketball jerseys, soccer kits, running singlets, baseball jerseys, volleyball uniforms, hockey jerseys and OEM/ODM design collections.

### 5. Cutting and Sewing
After printing and material preparation, fabric panels are cut and assembled according to product requirements. The cutting and sewing stage focuses on panel alignment, size consistency, seam construction, neckline and armhole structure, sleeve and hem finishing, and product-specific fit requirements.

### 6. Name and Number Customization
Many teamwear orders require player names, numbers and team identity details. POXIOL supports custom name and number placement according to sport category and buyer requirements.

### 7. Quality Control Before Packing
POXIOL QC review may include print color and pattern check, logo placement review, name and number accuracy, stitching and construction check, size and quantity verification, fabric surface check, product pairing check, packing and labeling check.

### 8. Packing and Shipment Preparation
Packing is part of the customer experience. Custom teamwear orders may be packed by size, player number, product set or buyer requirement.
