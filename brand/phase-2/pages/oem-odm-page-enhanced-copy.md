## OEM vs ODM at POXIOL

### OEM Support
OEM support is suitable when the buyer already has a product concept, reference style, logo, design file or brand direction. POXIOL can help turn that direction into a custom teamwear product through sampling and production support.

Common OEM needs include private label uniforms, existing design adaptation, logo and label placement, buyer-provided color direction, bulk production planning and product set development.

### ODM Support
ODM support is suitable when the buyer wants POXIOL to help develop product concepts, design direction or teamwear collection structure.

Common ODM needs include original design concept, product range planning, sport category expansion, fabric and color suggestions, collection mockups, sample development and seasonal teamwear programs.

## Development Process

### 1. Brand and Product Brief
The process starts with understanding your target market, buyer type, product category, price range, design style and order plan.

### 2. Product and Collection Planning
POXIOL can help organize your product plan into a clear collection structure, such as basketball jersey plus shorts, soccer jersey plus shorts and socks, running singlet plus shorts, training jacket plus pants or a multi-sport school collection.

### 3. Mockup and Design Direction
POXIOL can help create a visual mockup based on your logo, colors, sport type and design direction.

### 4. Sample Development
After design direction is confirmed, sample development helps verify fabric, fit, construction, printing effect and product details.

### 5. Production Planning
Once samples are approved, POXIOL supports production planning based on order quantity, product type, size range, customization complexity and target delivery timeline.

### 6. Packing and Delivery Support
For brand and distributor orders, packing requirements may include size grouping, product set grouping, label planning or buyer-specific packing instructions.
