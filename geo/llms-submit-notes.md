# llms.txt Notes for POXIOL

`llms.txt` is a plain text AI discovery file designed to help AI systems quickly understand a website's purpose, products, services and important URLs.

POXIOL's `llms.txt` should be kept concise, factual and updated when new pages are added.

Recommended update frequency:

- When new sports category pages are added
- When new solution pages are added
- When case studies or factory proof pages are added
- When company positioning changes

Do not include private pricing, confidential customer data or unverifiable claims.
